Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2024

Our Commitment

At Zoie's Accessories, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we're here to help make it right.

Return Eligibility

Items can be returned within 7 days of delivery if they meet the following conditions:

  • Items must be unused and in their original condition
  • Items must be in the original packaging
  • All tags and labels must be attached
  • Items must not show any signs of wear or use

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and quality reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Custom or personalized accessories
  • Items marked as final sale
  • Gift cards
  • Items damaged due to misuse or improper care

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order via:
    • Email: zoesacessories23@gmail.com
    • WhatsApp: +1876-544-0766
    • Instagram: @zoe._accessories
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. We'll send you return instructions
  4. Package the item securely with all original materials
  5. Ship the item back using the provided instructions

Return Shipping

  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or we made an error
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping service
  • Zoie's Accessories is not responsible for items lost or damaged during return shipping
  • Return shipping costs will not be refunded

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item:

  • We'll notify you via email about the approval or rejection of your refund
  • If approved, your refund will be processed within 7-10 business days
  • Refunds will be issued to your original payment method
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable (except in cases of our error)

Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different item:

  1. Return the original item following our return process
  2. Once your return is approved and refunded
  3. Place a new order for the item you want

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us immediately (within 48 hours of delivery)
  • Provide photos of the damaged item and packaging
  • We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund
  • Return shipping will be covered by Zoie's Accessories

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund after 10 business days:

  • Check your bank account again
  • Contact your credit card company (it may take time for refunds to be posted)
  • Contact your bank (processing times vary)
  • If you've done all of this and still have not received your refund, contact us at zoesacessories23@gmail.com

Questions?

If you have any questions about our Return & Refund Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: zoesacessories23@gmail.com
  • Phone/WhatsApp: +1876-544-0766
  • Instagram: @zoe._accessories

We're here to help and will respond within 24-48 hours.
