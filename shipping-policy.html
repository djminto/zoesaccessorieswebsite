Shipping Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2024

Free Shipping

We're excited to offer FREE SHIPPING on all orders within Jamaica! No minimum purchase required.

Shipping Locations

We currently ship to all parishes in Jamaica:

  • Kingston & St. Andrew
  • St. Catherine
  • Clarendon
  • Manchester
  • St. Elizabeth
  • Westmoreland
  • Hanover
  • St. James
  • Trelawny
  • St. Ann
  • St. Mary
  • Portland
  • St. Thomas

International shipping is not currently available.

Delivery Times

Delivery times vary depending on your location:

  • Kingston & St. Andrew: 1-3 business days
  • St. Catherine & Surrounding Areas: 2-4 business days
  • Other Parishes: 3-7 business days

Note: These are estimated delivery times. Actual delivery may vary depending on:

  • Order processing time
  • Courier availability
  • Weather conditions
  • Public holidays
  • Remote or difficult-to-access locations

Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation.

  • Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day
  • You will receive an email confirmation once your order is processed
  • Custom or personalized items may require additional processing time (3-5 business days)

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive:

  • A shipping confirmation email
  • Estimated delivery date
  • Contact information for tracking inquiries

For order status updates, you can:

  • Email us at: zoesacessories23@gmail.com
  • WhatsApp us at: +1876-544-0766
  • Message us on Instagram: @zoe._accessories

Delivery Methods

We use reliable courier services to ensure your order arrives safely:

  • Local courier services for Kingston & St. Andrew
  • Trusted delivery partners for other parishes
  • Hand delivery available for select areas (contact us for details)

Delivery Requirements

To ensure successful delivery:

  • Provide a complete and accurate shipping address
  • Include a working phone number for delivery contact
  • Someone must be available to receive the package
  • Provide clear directions for difficult-to-find locations

Failed Delivery Attempts

If delivery cannot be completed:

  • The courier will attempt to contact you using the phone number provided
  • Multiple delivery attempts may be made
  • You may need to arrange pickup or provide alternative delivery instructions
  • Orders not collected within 7 days may be returned and subject to restocking fees

Package Safety

All orders are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive in perfect condition:

  • Products are wrapped securely
  • Fragile items receive extra protection
  • Discreet packaging is used for all deliveries
  • Branded packaging for a special unboxing experience

Lost or Damaged Packages

In the rare event that your package is lost or arrives damaged:

  • Contact us immediately at zoesacessories23@gmail.com
  • Provide your order number and photos of any damage
  • We will work with the courier to resolve the issue
  • A replacement or refund will be issued as appropriate

Important: Report any damage within 48 hours of delivery.

Address Changes

Need to change your shipping address?

  • Contact us as soon as possible if you need to update your address
  • Address changes can only be made before the order is shipped
  • Once shipped, address changes may not be possible
  • Additional fees may apply for address changes after shipping

Holidays and Peak Seasons

During busy periods, please allow extra time for delivery:

  • Christmas season (December)
  • Valentine's Day
  • Mother's Day
  • Back to School season

We recommend ordering early during these times to ensure timely delivery.
